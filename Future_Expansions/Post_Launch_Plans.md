# Post-Launch Plans — Safety Code Violator

**Safety Code Violator GDD**
*Post-launch roadmap, monetization approach, and community goals. All content in the full version is planned for launch — this document covers what comes after.*

---

## Important Framing Notes

- **No DLC is planned.** Safety Code Violator is a single standalone game. All 40+ workplace environments are part of the base full version.
- **Post-launch content** consists of free updates, patches, and cosmetic additions only.
- **Monetization** after launch is through cosmetic packs only — no paid content gates gameplay.
- Specific pricing is a business decision made closer to launch. Not confirmed in this document.

---

## Phase 1 — Launch Window (First 3 Months)

Primary focus: stability, performance, and community foundation.

- Bug fixes and performance optimization — Android especially, given mid-range device targets
- Balance tuning based on player feedback — star rating thresholds, PD accumulation rates, placement budget growth
- Steam Achievements implementation — per-milestone and career-wide achievements
- Steam Leaderboards — per-workplace and global, Chaos Score + Plausibility Rating + Viral Impact
- Basic Steam Workshop support — highlight reel sharing and community viewing
- Community Challenge Shifts — optional weekly curated scenarios with preset conditions, bonus stars

---

## Phase 2 — First Major Free Update

Content additions based on what players engage with most.

- New worker archetype additions from Phase 3 implementation list (The Lucky One, The Unlucky One, The Conspiracy Theorist, The Sleepwalker, The Health and Safety Rep)
- Improved highlight reel system — better moment detection, more debrief screen detail
- Additional level variant conditions based on player feedback
- Expanded cosmetic pack — new sound packs, slow-motion filters, debrief screen themes
- Social sharing integration — highlight reel export to video for Twitter/X, TikTok, YouTube

---

## Phase 3 — Steam Workshop Full Integration

Full modding support for the PC version.

- Full Steam Workshop integration — custom workplace maps, hazard packs, worker personality packs, cosmetic resource packs
- Level editor tools — allow players to design and share custom workplace layouts
- Hazard creator — allow custom hazard definitions with custom assets and behavior scripts
- Player-created location contest — community-submitted workplaces reviewed for potential official inclusion
- Workshop curation system — featured and community-rated content

---

## Stretch Goals

*Features worth building toward if the game performs well. Not confirmed for any specific timeframe.*

**Multiplayer / Co-op**
Two players simultaneously setting up traps in the same workplace. Potential for cooperative (shared PD meter, combined chaos) or competitive (separate trap budgets, competing for highest score) modes. Non-trivial implementation — post-launch only if core game succeeds.

**Endless / Procedural Mode**
Procedurally generated mega-buildings combining elements from multiple workplace types. Infinite replay potential. Requires robust procedural generation system — significant scope addition.

**Full Replay Editor**
Editable highlight reels with manual camera control, custom slow-motion points, and caption editing. Export as shareable video file. Strong community and viral marketing value.

**Additional Platform Support**
iOS support if Android version performs well. PC controller support if there's demand. Neither confirmed for launch.

---

## Monetization Approach

**Base game:** Single purchase, full content. Pricing TBD closer to launch.

**Post-launch cosmetic packs (paid):**
These are purely aesthetic — no gameplay content gated behind purchase. All cosmetics can also be unlocked through play via the progression system (Track 4).

Examples of paid cosmetic pack content:
- Additional sound packs (Evil Laugh variants, Corporate Jargon, Nature Documentary narration, etc.)
- Additional slow-motion filter styles
- Additional debrief screen themes
- Additional UI flair packs

**Free updates:** All gameplay content, new archetypes, balance patches, and Workshop support are free updates.

**What is never paid:**
- Hazards or trap types
- Workplace environments
- Worker archetypes
- Progression track expansion
- Any content that affects scoring or gameplay

---

## Community Goals

- **Steam Workshop** — strong modding community with custom workplaces and hazard packs
- **Weekly Community Challenge Shifts** — curated optional scenarios, rotated weekly
- **Highlight reel sharing** — native export to social platforms, community viewing on Steam Workshop
- **Player-created location contest** — community-submitted workplaces with winning entries added officially as free updates
- **Leaderboards per workplace** — ongoing competitive layer for players who have mastered specific environments
