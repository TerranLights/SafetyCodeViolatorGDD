# Office
This will be the player’s first real playground and the most common early-game location.

**The starting location and tutorial hub.**

The classic open-plan office — the perfect playground for subtle, creative sabotage.

## Location Overview
- Medium size (one floor, ~25–35 workers)
- Multiple interconnected areas: open desks, meeting rooms, break room/kitchen, supply closet, elevators/stairs
- Good variety of hazards while remaining manageable for new players

## Key Areas & Opportunities

### Open-Plan Workspace
- Desks, chairs, monitors, power strips, filing cabinets
- High traffic = excellent chain reaction potential
- Classic combos: wet floor + rolling chairs + sharp desk corners

### Break Room / Kitchen
- Coffee machine (most popular target)
- Microwave, fridge, water cooler
- High density of tired, distracted workers

### Meeting Rooms
- Projectors, whiteboards, uncomfortable chairs
- Perfect for “team-building” disasters

### Supply Closet & Server Room
- Electrical panels, overloaded sockets, unstable shelving
- High-lethality potential but higher suspicion risk

## Typical Contract Objectives (Examples)
- “Improve productivity by removing unnecessary breaks”
- “Cut costs on office supplies and furniture”
- “Renovate the break room to be more ‘modern’”
- Bonus: “Make it look like a single faulty batch of ergonomic chairs was responsible”

## Difficulty & Learning Curve
- **Beginner friendly** — forgiving plausibility thresholds
- Teaches core mechanics: hazard placement, combo building, meter management
- First 3–5 contracts usually take place here before unlocking the Factory

## Unique Flavor
- Lots of ironic deaths (“Death by quarterly KPI report”)
- Strong corporate satire opportunities
- Workers have office-specific personalities (interns, middle managers, HR, etc.)

## Unlocks After Mastery
- Larger multi-floor office building
- Executive suite variant (higher budget, stricter suspicion)
- “After-Hours” mode (different worker routines)
