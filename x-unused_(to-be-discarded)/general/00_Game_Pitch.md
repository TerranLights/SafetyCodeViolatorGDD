# 00_Game_Pitch

## Steam Under-300-Character Description
Live your dream as the exact opposite of an OSHA Officer by making workplaces as utterly dangerous as possible!

## One-Liner
**Safety Code Violator** is a darkly comedic real-time simulation game where you play as a malicious “Safety Consultant” whose real job is to turn ordinary workplaces into hilarious deathtraps while maintaining perfect plausible deniability.

## High Concept
You are hired by shady corporations to “improve workplace safety” and cut costs. In reality, you are a professional chaos agent. Your goal is to cause maximum carnage, injuries, and fatalities in the most creative ways possible — all while making sure it looks like a series of unfortunate, unrelated accidents.

## Core Fantasy
- Being an evil genius who gets away with it
- Watching beautifully orchestrated chain reactions unfold
- The thrill of staying just barely under the radar
- Corporate satire and absurd dark humor

## Target Platforms
- **Primary**: Steam (Windows + Linux Native)
- **Secondary**: Android (mobile-friendly sessions of 15–30 minutes)

## Core Gameplay Loop (15–30 minutes per session)

1. **Briefing** (1–2 min)  
   Receive a new contract with suspicious objectives and a budget.

2. **Setup Phase** (5–8 min)  
   Place hazards, sabotage equipment, change workflows, and prepare the environment.

3. **Simulation Phase** (8–15 min)  
   Start the shift and watch the chaos unfold in real-time.  
   Speed up time, intervene when needed, and manage the **Plausible Deniability Meter**.

4. **Debrief** (2–3 min)  
   Review highlight reel, final score, statistics, and unlock new content.

## Key Selling Points
- Highly creative, systemic trap design and combo potential
- Strong replayability through different locations and contract types
- Smart worker AI with unique personalities
- Perfect blend of planning and real-time spectacle
- Hilarious dark humor and corporate satire
- Great touch controls for Android + mouse/keyboard on PC

## Tone & Style
- Darkly comedic, absurd, and satirical
- Cartoonish violence (think *Happy Tree Friends* meets *Untitled Goose Game*)
- No gore porn — focus on slapstick and ironic deaths

## Target Audience
- Fans of simulation games (*The Sims*, *Hitman*, *Poly Bridge*)
- Players who enjoy chaotic mischief games (*Untitled Goose Game*, *Goat Simulator*)
- People who love dark humor and corporate satire
