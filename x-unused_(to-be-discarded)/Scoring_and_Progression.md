# Scoring_and_Progression
This scoring system encourages players to be smart and creative instead of just spamming the most lethal traps.

## Overview
Scoring in *Safety Code Violator* rewards **creativity, efficiency, and plausible deniability** — not just raw body count.

## Main Scoring Categories

### 1. Chaos Score (Main Score)
- **Injuries & Fatalities** (weighted)
- **Chain Reaction Multipliers** (longer chains = bigger bonus)
- **Creativity Bonus** (using varied hazards and clever combos)
- **Style / Irony Bonus** (especially funny or thematically appropriate deaths)

### 2. Plausibility Rating (0–100%)
- How well you stayed under the radar
- Major multiplier for final score
- 100% plausibility = huge bonus
- Below 30% = heavy score penalty + risk of failing the contract

### 3. Contract Objectives
- Primary Objective (required)
- Secondary / Bonus Objectives (optional, high reward)
- Examples:
  - “Deny at least 5 insurance claims”
  - “Make it look like a single faulty batch of equipment”
  - “No deaths before lunch break”

### 4. Viral Impact
- How shareable / meme-worthy the accidents were
- Unlocks special rewards and cosmetic items

## Progression System

### Violator Rank
- Starts at **Junior Violator**
- Ranks up based on average score across contracts
- Higher rank unlocks:
  - More powerful / subtle hazards
  - Bigger and more complex locations
  - Advanced tools (timers, remote triggers, scapegoat mechanics)
  - Special “Signature” traps

### Unlock Types
- **Hazards & Tools**
- **Locations** (Office → Factory → Restaurant → Construction Site → Chemical Plant → High-Rise, etc.)
- **Worker Personality Packs**
- **Cosmetic / Flair** (evil laugh sound packs, dramatic slow-mo filters, etc.)
- **Contract Types** (harder objectives, tighter budgets, stricter investigators)

## Replayability Features
- **Star Rating** per contract (1–5 stars)
- **Challenge Contracts** (weekly or special)
- **Perfect Run** achievements (100% plausibility + high chaos)
- **Leaderboards** (Steam) for most creative deaths

## End-of-Shift Score Screen Highlights
- Top 5 funniest moments
- “Most Valuable Victim” award
- “Best Combo” breakdown
- Total Insurance Money “Saved” for the company
