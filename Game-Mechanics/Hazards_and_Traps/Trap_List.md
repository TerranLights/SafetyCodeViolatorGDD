# Trap_List

## Overview
This is the master list of hazards/traps. They are grouped by category and include key stats for design reference.

### Hazard Stats Legend
- **Cost**: Budget points required
- **Suspicion**: How much it raises the Plausible Deniability meter
- **Lethality**: Base chance of injury/fatality
- **Combo Potential**: How well it works with other hazards

## Basic Hazards (Early Game)

### Environmental
- Wet Floor (no sign) — Cost: 1 | Suspicion: Low | Lethality: Low | Combo: Excellent
- Loose Cables — Cost: 2 | Suspicion: Low-Medium | Lethality: Medium | Combo: High
- Unstable Shelving — Cost: 3 | Suspicion: Medium | Lethality: Medium-High | Combo: Very High
- Poor Lighting — Cost: 1 | Suspicion: Very Low | Lethality: Low | Combo: High

### Equipment
- Faulty Coffee Machine — Cost: 4 | Suspicion: Low | Lethality: Medium | Combo: Legendary
- Overloaded Power Strip — Cost: 3 | Suspicion: Medium | Lethality: Medium-High | Combo: High
- Wobbly Ladder — Cost: 2 | Suspicion: Low | Lethality: Medium | Combo: Excellent
- Broken Safety Harness — Cost: 5 | Suspicion: Medium-High | Lethality: High | Combo: High

### Policy & Workflow
- Removed Safety Training — Cost: 2 | Suspicion: Very Low | Lethality: Low | Combo: Medium
- Mandatory Overtime — Cost: 3 | Suspicion: Low | Lethality: Medium | Combo: High
- Encouraged "Multi-Tasking" — Cost: 2 | Suspicion: Low | Lethality: Low-Medium | Combo: High

## Advanced Hazards (Mid → Late Game)

### Industrial
- Faulty Conveyor Belt — Cost: 6 | Suspicion: Medium | Lethality: High
- Crane Brake Failure — Cost: 8 | Suspicion: High | Lethality: Very High
- Chemical Mislabeling — Cost: 7 | Suspicion: High | Lethality: Very High

### Creative / High-Combo
- "Ergonomic" Chair with Faulty Wheels — Cost: 4 | Suspicion: Low | Lethality: Medium
- Fake "Team Building" Exercise on Unsafe Equipment
- "Cost-Cutting" Replacement of Fire Suppressors with Water
- Malfunctioning Walk-In Freezer Lock

## Special Signature Traps (Unlockable)
- The Kevin Special (coffee machine + wet floor + rolling chair combo)
- The Karen Nemesis (HR worker targeted with ironic death)
- The "Totally Legitimate Maintenance" chain

## Design Rules
- Every hazard must have a believable corporate excuse
- Stronger hazards generally have higher suspicion
- The best traps are usually combinations of 3+ smaller ones
- Visual and audio feedback must be extremely satisfying
