# Construction_Site
This location excels at delivering **big, cinematic, screen-filling chaos** and will create some of the most shareable highlight reels.

**High-stakes, large-scale chaos with heavy machinery and gravity as your best friend.**

A bustling construction site. Tall scaffolding, heavy equipment, and workers operating at dangerous heights make this one of the most spectacular locations.

## Location Overview
- Large outdoor/indoor hybrid site
- ~60–100 workers (builders, welders, crane operators, supervisors)
- Multiple floors/levels under construction
- Extremely high lethality and visual impact

## Key Areas & Opportunities

### Scaffolding & Upper Levels
- Unstable scaffolding, missing guardrails, loose planks
- Falling objects from above

### Heavy Machinery Zone
- Cranes, excavators, bulldozers, cement mixers
- "Maintenance issues" with brakes, hydraulics, or operators

### Ground Level / Material Storage
- Unsecured stacks of bricks, steel beams, rebar
- Potholes, wet concrete, open manholes
- Temporary power generators and fuel storage

### Site Office & Break Area
- Smaller enclosed space for more personal sabotage
- Good for contrast with the large-scale outdoor chaos

## Typical Contract Objectives (Examples)
- “Accelerate construction schedule by 30%”
- “Reduce safety equipment costs”
- “Improve worker efficiency through new scheduling”
- Bonus: “Make it look like a ‘freak weather incident’ or ‘worker negligence’”

## Difficulty & Characteristics
- **High difficulty**
- Massive chain reaction potential (one falling beam can take out many)
- Very high visual spectacle — perfect for dramatic slow-motion highlights
- Harder to control plausibility due to the scale and number of witnesses

## Unique Flavor & Humor
- Over-the-top slapstick construction disasters
- Workers yelling “Watch out!” right before ironically dying
- “It’s not safety regulations, it’s just common sense” satire
- Glorious slow-motion falls from great heights

## Progression & Variants
- Unlocks after proving yourself in Restaurant and Factory
- Later variants:
  - Skyscraper construction (multi-floor vertical chaos)
  - Demolition site (even higher destruction potential)
  - Luxury high-rise (richer targets, stricter oversight)
