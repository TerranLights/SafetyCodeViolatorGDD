# Factory
This location marks the transition from “subtle office mischief” to “glorious industrial carnage.”

**The second major location — bigger, louder, and much more dangerous.**

A classic industrial factory floor. Heavy machinery, assembly lines, and lots of tired workers in a high-risk environment.

## Location Overview
- Large open floor with multiple assembly lines
- High worker density (~50–80 workers per shift)
- Many heavy industrial machines and moving parts
- Higher base lethality but also higher scrutiny

## Key Areas & Opportunities

### Main Assembly Lines
- Conveyor belts, robotic arms, stamping machines
- Excellent for timing-based chain reactions
- Workers repeating the same motions = predictable (and exploitable) behavior

### Break Room & Locker Area
- Still important, but less central than in the office
- Shift changes create natural traffic spikes

### Loading Dock & Warehouse Section
- Forklifts, heavy crates, unstable stacking
- Chemical storage (oils, solvents, gases)

### Maintenance & Electrical Rooms
- High-voltage panels, overhead cranes, pressurized systems
- Extremely high lethality + high suspicion risk

## Typical Contract Objectives (Examples)
- “Increase production output by 25%”
- “Cut maintenance budget by 40%”
- “Modernize the safety systems” (ironic)
- Bonus: “Make it look like worker error / fatigue instead of equipment failure”

## Difficulty & Characteristics
- **Medium difficulty** — bigger scale means more chaos potential but harder to control suspicion
- More opportunities for spectacular, multi-victim chain reactions
- Noisier and more chaotic atmosphere (great for sound design)

## Unique Flavor & Humor
- Industrial slapstick (crushed by steel beams, mangled by machines, toxic chemical “accidents”)
- Union vs Management satire
- Workers yelling safety slogans right before horrific deaths

## Progression & Variants
- Unlocks after completing several Office contracts
- Later variants:
  - Night shift (different lighting, fewer workers, higher fatigue)
  - Automated factory (more robots, different hazard types)
  - Chemical processing plant (advanced unlock)
