# Future_Locations
This acts as a living roadmap for content expansion.

## Overview
This document tracks planned future locations as the game expands. These will be unlocked progressively as the player ranks up.

## Planned Locations (In Rough Order)

### 1. Chemical Processing Plant
- Toxic chemicals, pressurized pipes, reactors
- Extremely high lethality and combo potential
- Very high suspicion baseline (strict safety regulations)

### 2. High-Rise Office Building (Multi-Floor)
- Multiple floors + elevators
- Vertical chain reactions (falling objects, elevator sabotage)
- Mix of office and construction hazards

### 3. Hospital
- Medical equipment, drugs, surgical tools, patients + staff
- Darker humor tone (ethical satire)
- Very high plausibility challenge

### 4. Amusement Park
- Rides, rollercoasters, food stalls, crowds
- Extremely visual and spectacular accidents
- Great for viral clip potential

### 5. Cruise Ship
- Multiple decks, confined spaces, engines, kitchens, pools
- “Trapped at sea” escalation
- Long shift length variant

### 6. Data Center / Server Farm
- Cooling systems, massive power infrastructure, delicate electronics
- “Everything must look like user error”

### 7. Mine / Underground Facility
- Darkness, heavy machinery, cave-ins, gas pockets
- Very high destruction potential

### 8. Space Station / Orbital Facility (Endgame)
- Zero-gravity chaos
- Ultimate challenge with limited resources and extreme consequences

## Design Philosophy for New Locations
- Each new location should introduce **new hazard categories** and **new worker types**
- Scale and complexity should increase gradually
- Each location must feel distinct both mechanically and humor-wise
- Strong visual identity and sound design for each

## Stretch Goals
- Seasonal/event locations (e.g. “Black Friday Superstore”, “Holiday Resort”)
- User-created locations via modding (Steam Workshop)
