# Restaurant
This location is excellent for **over-the-top, visually funny** accidents and will likely produce some of the best viral highlight clips.

**A fast-paced, high-pressure location full of heat, knives, and stressed staff.**

Busy commercial kitchen + dining area. Tight spaces, hot surfaces, and lots of sharp objects make this one of the most chaotic and funniest locations.

## Location Overview
- Medium-large size split between **Kitchen** and **Dining Area**
- ~30–50 workers (cooks, waitstaff, bussers, managers)
- High traffic and constant movement
- Very high lethality potential due to fire, sharp tools, and hot liquids

## Key Areas & Opportunities

### Commercial Kitchen
- Multiple stoves, deep fryers, ovens, and grills
- Knives, slippery floors from oil/grease, heavy pots
- Walk-in freezer and food storage areas

### Dining Area
- Tables, chairs, slippery floors, heavy chandeliers or ceiling fans
- Customer area (adds extra civilians for bonus chaos)

### Back-of-House
- Dishwashing station, trash compactor, delivery entrance
- Electrical panels and gas lines

## Typical Contract Objectives (Examples)
- “Improve kitchen efficiency and reduce wait times”
- “Cut costs on equipment maintenance and staff training”
- “Renovate the dining area to be more ‘modern and open-concept’”
- Bonus: “Make it look like a single bad batch of cooking oil was responsible”

## Difficulty & Characteristics
- **Medium-High difficulty**
- Very high combo potential (fire + oil + rushing staff = spectacular results)
- Tight spaces make chain reactions feel more intense
- Fast-paced — things escalate quickly once the first accident happens

## Unique Flavor & Humor
- Extremely slapstick and messy deaths (burned, stabbed, crushed by falling plates, slipped on grease)
- “This is fine” chef memes
- Angry customers, food critics, and health inspectors as extra pressure
- Glorious yelling in the kitchen right before disaster

## Progression & Variants
- Unlocks after several successful Factory or Office contracts
- Later variants:
  - Fine dining restaurant (higher budget, richer victims)
  - Fast food chain (higher volume, lower quality equipment)
  - Michelin-starred nightmare (very high suspicion baseline)
