# 02_Tone_and_Humor
This file sets the emotional and comedic boundaries for the entire project.

## Overall Tone
**Darkly comedic, satirical, and absurd.**

The game leans into the ridiculousness of corporate culture while letting the player be gloriously, unapologetically evil. It should feel like a mix of *Untitled Goose Game*, *Happy Tree Friends*, and *The Office* — but with lethal consequences.

## Humor Styles Used

### 1. Corporate Satire
- Over-the-top buzzwords and corporate doublespeak ("rightsizing", "synergizing", "improving workplace wellness")
- Absurd performance metrics ("Synergy Score", "Team Spirit Index", "Insurance Claim Denial Rate")
- Emails and briefings from upper management that completely ignore the rising body count

### 2. Slapstick & Irony
- A worker slipping on a wet floor... that you "accidentally" placed right under a shelf of heavy boxes
- Someone proudly using the new "ergonomic" chair you sabotaged
- The safety officer who keeps narrowly avoiding every trap until the final glorious combo

### 3. Black Comedy
- Workers dramatically monologuing right before their demise
- Creative death descriptions in the debrief ("Crushed by quarterly reports", "Death by stapler")
- Viral news headlines and social media reactions after big incidents

### 4. Character-Driven Humor
- Workers have distinct personalities that make their deaths funnier:
  - Kevin the intern who keeps trying to "fix" your sabotage
  - Karen from HR who reports everything
  - Chad the sales guy who thinks he’s invincible

## Content Guidelines

**Allowed:**
- Cartoonish / slapstick violence
- Dark humor about death and injury
- Corporate evil and greed
- Absurd situations

**Avoided (for now):**
- Extreme gore or realistic suffering
- Sexual content
- Real-world political commentary (keep it general corporate satire)

## Visual & Audio Tone
- Bright, clean, colorful art style (to contrast with the dark actions)
- Cheerful corporate background music that becomes increasingly unhinged as chaos rises
- Satisfying cartoon sound effects for deaths and chain reactions
- Dramatic slow-motion with funny voice-over commentary during highlight reels
