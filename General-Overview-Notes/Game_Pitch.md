## Steam Under-300-Character Description

Live your dream as the exact opposite of an OSHA Officer by making workplaces as utterly dangerous as possible!

---

## One-Liner

**Safety Code Violator** is a darkly comedic real-time simulation game where you play as a malicious "Safety Consultant" whose real job is to turn ordinary workplaces into hilarious deathtraps while maintaining perfect plausible deniability.

---

## High Concept

You are the exact opposite of an OSHA Officer. Your goal is to cause maximum carnage, injuries, and fatalities in the most creative ways possible — all while making sure it looks like a series of unfortunate, unrelated accidents. The workplace starts clean, orderly, and safe. By the end of your shift, it should be neither.

---

## Core Fantasy

- Being an evil genius who keeps getting away with it
- Watching beautifully orchestrated chain reactions unfold in real time
- The thrill of staying just barely under the radar
- Corporate satire and absurd dark humor taken to their logical extreme
- "I can't believe that actually worked"

## Key Selling Points

- Deep, creative, systemic hazard placement with massive combo potential
- Strong replayability — 40+ workplace environments, unlimited shifts, milestone-based mastery
- Smart worker AI with distinct personality archetypes that create emergent comedy
- Perfect blend of planning (setup phase) and real-time spectacle (simulation phase)
- Hilarious dark humor and corporate satire — deadpan presentation of increasingly absurd situations
- Great touch controls for Android, precise mouse/keyboard on PC
- No prescribed goals — pure sandbox, score emerges from how well you play

---

## Tone & Style

- Darkly comedic, absurd, and satirical
- Cartoonish slapstick violence — think *Happy Tree Friends* meets *Untitled Goose Game*
- No gore — focus on slapstick, irony, and absurdist humor
- The game treats devastating workplace incidents as productivity metrics
- Corporate deadpan presentation of completely unhinged events

---

## Target Audience

- Fans of simulation and sandbox games (*The Sims*, *Hitman*, *Poly Bridge*)
- Players who enjoy chaotic mischief games (*Untitled Goose Game*, *Goat Simulator*)
- People who love dark humor and corporate satire
- Mobile players who want a rich, replayable game in 15–30 minute sessions
- PC players who want a creative sandbox with depth and variety
