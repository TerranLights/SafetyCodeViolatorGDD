# Sample_Level_01_Office
This level serves as the player’s true first “I caused that!” moment and should feel satisfying and funny.

**First real contract — Tutorial / Onboarding Level**

## Contract Title
“Floor 3 Renovation – Cost Cutting Initiative”

## Briefing Text
> “The company wants to reduce overhead on the third floor. We’ve been told to cut costs by 35% while ‘boosting team synergy.’ Make it happen. Bonus if we can write off a few insurance claims as ‘freak accidents.’”

## Location
Standard Open-Plan Office (small tutorial version)

## Budget
Moderate (enough for 8–12 basic hazards)

## Primary Objectives
- Cause at least 6 injuries / 2 fatalities
- Keep Plausible Deniability above 40% at end of shift
- Make at least one accident look like a single faulty piece of equipment

## Secondary Objectives (Bonus)
- Take out Kevin the Intern in a spectacular way
- Cause a chain reaction involving the coffee machine
- Keep suspicion under 70% the entire shift

## Key Hazards Available
- Wet Floor signs (missing)
- Faulty rolling chairs
- Overloaded power strips
- Unstable filing cabinets
- Broken coffee machine
- Removed safety posters

## Worker Personalities (Notable)
- **Kevin the Intern** – Clumsy + Overachiever (prime target)
- **Karen from HR** – Paranoid + Gossip
- **Chad Sales** – Distracted + Cocky
- Generic workers with basic traits

## Teaching Goals (Hidden)
- Introduce hazard placement
- Teach basic combo building
- Show how the Plausible Deniability meter works
- Demonstrate time speed controls
- First highlight reel

## Difficulty
Very Easy – Forgiving plausibility thresholds

## Success Rewards
- Unlock 2 new basic hazards
- Unlock Factory location teaser
- “Junior Violator” rank promotion
