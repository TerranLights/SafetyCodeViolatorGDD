## Example Templates

### 1. Basic Cost-Cutting (Early Game)
**Title:** “Q3 Efficiency Drive”  
**Budget:** Low  
**Primary:**
- Cut costs by 30%+ through “maintenance optimization”
- At least 4 injuries / 1 fatality  
**Secondary:**
- Make it look like a single faulty equipment batch
- Take out at least one middle manager

### 2. Productivity Push (Medium)
**Title:** “Mandatory Team Synergy Initiative”  
**Budget:** Medium  
**Primary:**
- Increase “productivity metrics” via new procedures
- Minimum 8 injuries / 3 fatalities  
**Secondary:**
- Coffee machine related incident
- Chain reaction of 4+ hazards

### 3. High-Risk Renovation (Late Game)
**Title:** “Floor 17 Modernization Project”  
**Budget:** High  
**Primary:**
- Complete “renovation” while denying all insurance claims
- Minimum 12 fatalities with high plausibility  
**Secondary:**
- Zero deaths before lunch break
- Viral clip potential (high spectacle)

### 4. Cover-Up Special
**Title:** “Cover Our Ass – Insurance Audit Incoming”  
**Budget:** Medium-High  
**Primary:**
- Extremely high plausibility required (>85%)
- Still cause significant damage  
**Secondary:**
- Frame a specific worker as the sole cause

## Random Modifiers (Add Flavor)
- Paranoid Safety Inspector on site
- Double budget but +20% starting suspicion
- All workers are extremely clumsy today
- “Bring Your Kid to Work Day” (extra civilians)
- Night shift (different routines, lower visibility)

## Usage Tips
- Mix Primary + Secondary + 1 Modifier per contract
- Scale difficulty by location + rank
- Use templates to maintain consistent quality and humor across levels
